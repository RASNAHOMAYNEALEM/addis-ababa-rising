Kazanchis: Where Memory Refuses to Die

This campaign is a living memorial. A resurrection. A justice call for those who built Addis Ababa with stone, song, sacrifice — only to be displaced. We remember. We rebuild.

They Built Homes, Not Just Houses

🏛 Fitawrari Desalegn's Stone Legacy

His home stood like a fortress in Kazanchis — carved in stone, passed through generations. They destroyed it. We will rebuild it in his name.

🎶 Tilahun Gessese’s "Felige Asfeligie"

The song was written in that home. His voice echoed through Libefana alleyways. They erased his street, but not his sound. His house rises again.

🏠 Grazmach Tasie's Menelik-Era House

It became a restaurant where history breathed. A Menelik-style architectural gem, later flattened. But we remember every brick — and will rebuild every line.

🛡 Ras Imru Haile Selassie’s Post-Italian Victory Home

In that humble structure, dignity returned after fascist invasion. The Prosperity Party erased it — but its spirit is eternal. Imru Heights will rise.
