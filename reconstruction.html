Rebuilding With Memory

Modern, sustainable housing that honors heritage and returns dignity.

Vision

We are creating new housing projects rooted in justice, technology, and cultural restoration. These buildings resettle displaced residents while reviving iconic heritage architecture.

Key Complexes

  • Felige Asfeligie: Inspired by Tilahun Gessese’s love home
  • Imru Heights: Echoes Ras Imru’s post-Italian era compound
  • Libe Fana: A modern revival of Fitawrari Atnafseged’s wood-and-glass residence
  • Tasie Corner: Menelik-era stone beauty once belonging to Grazmach Tassie

Features

  • Eco-friendly solar power & water systems
  • Earthquake-resistant smart design
  • Public gardens, heritage murals, community halls

Community First

We aim to return every resident to their neighborhood. These homes are not for profit — they are for memory, dignity, and justice.
