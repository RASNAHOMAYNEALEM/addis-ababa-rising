International Legal Case

This campaign is anchored in a formal complaint titled "Kazanchis: Cultural Erasure & Crimes Against Humanity in Addis Ababa", submitted to the African Commission, UN OHCHR, ICC, and UNESCO.

It documents systemic state violence, displacement, and destruction inflicted on the people of Kazanchis and wider Addis Ababa under the Prosperity Party regime. It is both a legal indictment and a historic record.
