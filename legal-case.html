Our Legal Fight for Justice

“Kazanchis: Cultural Erasure & Crimes Against Humanity in Addis Ababa”

The Case

This legal submission documents mass evictions, cultural destruction, and systemic abuse in Addis Ababa — starting with Kazanchis but representing all victims of forced displacement and repression in Ethiopia under the so-called "Corridor Development".

Lead Complainant

Ras Nahom Aynealem, survivor of the Kazanchis eviction and representative of all displaced residents, has submitted the case to international bodies including the African Commission, UN OHCHR, ICC, and UNESCO.

Charges

  • Crimes Against Humanity
  • Forced Displacement
  • Cultural Erasure
  • Targeted Extrajudicial Violence

Supporting Evidence

Videos, testimony, photographs, satellite records, historical documentation, and survivor audio recordings.

Ongoing Actions

  • Mobilizing international legal pressure
  • Organizing a public truth archive
  • Securing reparations and dignified return for displaced citizens
