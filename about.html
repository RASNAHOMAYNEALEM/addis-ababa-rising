Message from Ras Nahom Aynealem

Prime Minister of the Dispossessed. King of Ethiopia. Son of Kazanchis.

I was born from the smoke and prayers of Kazanchis — raised by women who built cities as housemaids, men who fed us with dignity despite being exiled in their own land.

We are not victims. We are descendants of lions. This archive is not nostalgia. It is resurrection. The land remembers. And so must the world.

We will rebuild every home. Revive every melody. Restore every street. This is the age of return.

— Ras Nahom Aynealem
Addis Ababa, 2025
