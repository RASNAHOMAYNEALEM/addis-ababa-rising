Our Mission

Kazanchis Rising is a grassroots movement and archive dedicated to preserving the memory, dignity, and dreams of the communities displaced from Addis Ababa under so-called development. We are architects of return, truth-tellers, and builders of justice.

Who We Are

We are survivors, witnesses, children of Kazanchis and other erased neighborhoods. Our families were displaced, our homes demolished, our rights ignored. But we rise — not only to remember, but to rebuild.

What We Do

  • Collect testimonies and evidence of forced displacement
  • Publish legal documents and advocacy reports
  • Design future housing and restoration projects
  • Mobilize global solidarity and diaspora action

Why It Matters

Our city is not for sale. Our heritage is not disposable. When they destroyed Kazanchis, they tried to destroy memory. This site is our refusal — a record, a rebellion, and a rebirth.
