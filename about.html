Our Mission

We are rebuilding Addis Ababa in spirit—addressing displacement, worshipping heritage, and reclaiming justice.

Who We Are

Survivors, artists, mothers, fighters—from Kazanchis to Piassa. We rise together to rewrite history.

What We Do

  • Document forced demolitions and displacement
  • Publish testimonies and evidence
  • Design future housing systems rooted in heritage
  • Gather global solidarity

Why It Matters

Our heritage isn’t disposable; memory is resistance. We are here to remember, resist, and rebuild.
