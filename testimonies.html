Here lies the truth in raw words — stories of loss, resistance, and resurrection from Kazanchis and beyond.

Solomon Tesfaye "እንግሽ" — Forever With Us

“They tore down our home without warning. Engish tried to reason with them… The next day, he was gone.”

— Neighbor and witness

The Night of Eviction

“They came with flashlights and loudspeakers. Children screamed. Old men begged. I still hear the sound of boots on gravel.”

— Audio witness, 28 Meskerem

The Kazanchis That Made It Through

“We survived Italians, Derg, hunger — but not Prosperity Party. They erased our homes, not just our houses.”

— Elder testimony from Urael Church

For the World to Know

“I don’t want pity. I want justice. I want them to see we were people — not slums to be deleted.”

— Woman survivor, evicted after Ahadu interview

“Human Sacrifices” — Ras Nahom

our mothers and fathers..
our history, legacy and memories,
our toughts, honor, ideas, love, fire and hope
demolished, destroyed n Scrubbed...
are we not Human??? or is there an unfavored type?
are we not ETHIOPIANS??? or are Ethiopians no more welcome in Ethiopia?
what are we fighting? what are we fighting for?
is whatever it is worth us?
why are we still dying after so many died for us?
if we are not living right
why were our lovers sacrificed?
For what?
For this?
forceful evictions?
persecution?
weaponised hunger and bulldozed dreams?
They tore our roofs while we cried and screamed
but we will not be erased we are returning.
and justice is coming with us.

— From the archives of the exiled
